----------------------------------------------------------------

ingredients 0.3.5
----------------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------------

* `feature_importance` now can take `variable_grouping` argument that assess importance of group of features

* fix [#27](https://github.com/ModelOriented/ingredients/issues/27) for multiple rows
